About the Daydreams


Daydreams

For this project, I created 10 daydreams from which I dreamed about for 15-25 minutes. The daydreams were created physically and digitally. I used items found around my house and some from purchased items from the store. For my background, I painted clouds and background using acryllic paints of various colors. This project was created as an inspiration to expand on the bedroom intervention & daydreaming assigned for Kimi Hanauer's Core Studio III.
